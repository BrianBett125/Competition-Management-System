Competition Management System

Effortlessly manage your competitions from start to finish.

Key Features

Easy Online Registration

This feature allows potential participants to register for the competition online easily. It eliminates the need for paper forms and streamlines the application process. Users can fill out information, upload required documents, and pay fees (if applicable) all in one place.

Streamlined Application Management

This feature helps competition organizers manage applications efficiently. They can view a list of all applicants, review individual profiles, download submitted documents, and easily communicate with participants through the system.

Secure Judging and Evaluation

This advanced feature allows organizers to set up secure judging processes. Judges can access and evaluate submissions anonymously, assign scores based on pre-defined criteria, and provide feedback electronically. This ensures a fair and transparent judging process.

About the Project

I recently participated in a Hackathon competition, and the experience was amazing! But managing registrations, deadlines, and communication for everyone involved became a huge hassle. It took me and my team five weeks to build this competition management system to streamline the entire process for future events. This is a Portfolio Project for Holberton School: https://www.holbertonschool.com/
